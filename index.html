Our Story

It seems almost unthinkable that a systems engineer like Fajutagana would venture into the cake shop business Sweet Solutions Trading in Imus, Cavite.

Leo’s shipmates in the navy would be totally surprised to find fim running a bakeshop. He used to be in charge of a team of 20 aviation mechanics and technicians tasked with keeping carrier-based F18 Hornet fighter jets in constant readiness.

From jets to cakes, where’s the connection? Leo, however, does not think his naval experience is far too removed from the business enterprise he’s involved in right now. In fact, he says that his navy experience has proven to be very helpful in business. The organizational, leadership and management skills he learned in the navy helped him to become a successful entrepreneur.
