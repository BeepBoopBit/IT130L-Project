FAQ

What sizes of cakes can I order?

There are 2 standard sizes, 8 round and 6 round but you can design and request size and number of layers.

Can I have the cake delivered?

Yes! We deliver within Cavite and some places in Metro Manila.

Do you have physical stores where I can pick up and shop?

Yes! Currently we operate 3 stores; 1. Imus, Cavite Bayan Luma 3, 2. Imus, Cavite Nueno, 3. Cavite City.

What can I add to my cake?

Anything! With Sweet Solutions, you can be the designer. Add and design anything from the number of candles to flavor, design, color, and more!

How can I order a wedding cake?

For special orders, please contact us directly to ensure that every detail is not missed.
